The Standard

Free to join.
Earned to stay in.

First Class doesn't cost money. It costs movement. Every member is expected to show up — not perfectly, but consistently. Share what you're building. Post your progress. Give real feedback. Go quiet for 30 days and the seat is gone.

This is not a policy. It's a feature. The standard is what makes the room worth being in.

"The question is not whether you can afford to be held accountable. The question is whether you can afford not to be."
01
Move Weekly
Post at least once a week. A win, a loss, a lesson, a question. Silence is not acceptable. Inactivity is the only way to lose your seat.
02
Give Real Feedback
When someone asks for feedback, tell them the truth. Not what they want to hear. Soft feedback is a waste of everyone's time.
03
No Spectators
You are not here to watch other people build. You have your own business to run. Show up for others — and show up for yours.
04
Document the Journey
Share the process — not just the wins. The losses, the pivots, the confusion. That's what actually helps everyone in the room.
05
Respect the Room
No noise. No spam. No self-promotion that doesn't add value. The room stays clean because everyone treats it like the asset it is.
06
Push the Needle
Every week, do something that moves your business forward. Doesn't have to be big. Has to be real. First Class is for people who don't stop.
